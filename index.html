Cultural Treasures

Afghan proverbs, known as "matals," have been the compass of society for centuries. These nuggets of wisdom, passed orally through generations, reflect the values, humor, and resilience of the Afghan people. Explore this collection to understand life through the lens of Afghan culture.

با صبر، ترشی هم شیرین میشود
"With patience, even vinegar becomes sweet"

Meaning

Patience can transform even the most difficult situations into positive outcomes

یک دست صدا ندارد
"One hand cannot clap alone"

Meaning

Cooperation and teamwork are essential for success
