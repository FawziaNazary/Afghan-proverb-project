خشت اول گر نهد معمار کج، تا ثریا میرود دیوار کج
"If the foundation is laid crooked, the wall will lean until paradise"

Detailed Explanation

This proverb emphasizes the importance of proper foundations. It suggests that small initial mistakes can lead to major problems later if not corrected early.

Cultural Context

Often used in education and child-rearing contexts, stressing the importance of good early training.
